About Us

About Global Tunnels

Global Tunnels Limited Company, incorporated in Virginia in 2003, is an information technology service company that provides custom programming, application infrastructure hosting, cybersecurity services, business process solutions, database management and financial management support for both government and commercial enterprises.

As a professional and innovative partner, our company brings sophisticated software solutions to the business needs of our customers. Working closely with our customers ensures that we understand their business, IT goals and objectives, and their processes for achieving them. We are then able to deliver efficient, cost-effective IT solutions to satisfy their requirements.

ContractVehicles

Global Tunnels was awarded IT Schedule 70 contract number GS-35F-0074V by the U.S. General Services Administration (GSA) in November 2008 and certified as an 8(a) small business vendor on February 25, 2015 by the Small Business Administration. Click here for how to order from the GSA Schedule.Global Tunnels was awarded GSA GWAC 8a Stars II contract in 2017.

The 8(a) STARS II contract is a small business set-aside GWAC providing access to Global Tunnels’ custom IT solutions. Under this contract, Global Tunnels offers fixed price, time and materials, labor hour, and blended task order types, providing greater flexibility in procuring IT service solutions to meet your mission requirements. The 8(a) STARS II allows directed task orders up to $4 Million, including options. Click here here for more information about how to order Global Tunnels’ services using GSA 8(a) Stars II.

Global Tunnels is certified as a small, minority-owned business (SWaM). The SWaM certification program is a state program of the Commonwealth of Virginia. Click here for more information about this program.
