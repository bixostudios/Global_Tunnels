Infrastructure as a Service (IaaS) and Platform as a Service (PaaS)

Global Tunnels focuses on providing web and application hosting platforms on either Amazon Cloud or on the client’s facility. Our service integrates security compliance, application performance and cost effectiveness. Highlights of our capabilities include:

  • Strategic and Tactical Planning and IT Planning
  • IT Infrastructure Library Process Analysis/ Assessment/ Reengineering/ Optimization
  • IT Security Planning, Implementation and Administration
  • Integration Service with Existing Infrastructure Components
  • Amazon Cloud as a Cloud Service Provider (CSP)
  • FedRAMP Compliance
  • Cloud-based Data and Infrastructure Disaster Recovery
  • IT Legacy System Modernization

Custom Computer Programming Service

Global Tunnels provides Software as a Service (SaaS), and full custom programming service throughout the systems development life cycle (SDLC) based on the customer’s requirements. Our product brings the highest efficiency, usability and sustainability (NAICS Codes: 541511 and 541512). Principal capabilities include:

  • Customer Requirement Analyses
  • System Design
  • Database Design and Management
  • IT Legacy system modernization
  • Identity Access Management Integration

Data Management Service

Global Tunnels provides comprehensive and complete life cycle data management services, including data collection or and creation, data processing, data validation and governance, data storage, data security, data transformation and data access. Our service brings the latest industry practices for creating, building, maintaining, and optimizing data management and long term data quality across the data lifecycle. This service can be applied to federal financial, budgetary, and other administrative data, or scientific research data (NAICS Codes: 518210, 541219 , 541611 and 921130). Our capabilities include:

  • Data Governance
  • Cloud Data Stoage and Use
  • Data Security for Storage and Transformation
  • Data Processing
  • Data Policy

Financial Accounting Service

Global Tunnels has experienced staff and CPA expertise for public accounting and federal budget formulation and execution. Our advantage is in problem resolution, process optimization and re-engineering (NAICS Codes: 541219 and 921130).
