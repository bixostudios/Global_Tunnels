Detailed Services

These solutions emerge from our thorough understanding of each customer’s mission, vision, cultural values, business requirements and processes.

IaaS & PaaS

Infrastructure as a Service (IaaS) and Platform as a Service (PaaS), Global Tunnels focuses on providing web and application hosting platforms on either Amazon Cloud or on the client’s facility. Our service integrates security compliance, application performance and cost effectiveness. (NAICS: 541512 and 541519)

Custom Computer Programming Service

Global Tunnels provides Software as a Service (SaaS), and full custom programming service throughout the systems development life cycle (SDLC) based on the customer’s requirements. Our product brings the highest efficiency, usability and sustainability. (NAICS: 541511 and 541512).

Data Management Service

Global Tunnels provides comprehensive and complete life cycle data management services, including data collection or and creation, data processing, data validation and governance, data storage, data security, data transformation and data access. Our service brings the latest industry practices for creating, building, maintaining, and optimizing data management and long term data quality across the data lifecycle. This service can be applied to federal financial, budgetary, and other administrative data, or scientific research data. (NAICS: 518210, 541219 , 541611 and 921130).

Financial Accounting Service

Global Tunnels has experienced staff and CPA expertise for public accounting and federal budget formulation and execution. Our advantage is in problem resolution, process optimization and re-engineering. (NAICS: 541219 and 921130)
